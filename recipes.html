Authentic African Recipes

Jollof Rice

Ingredients: Rice, tomatoes, onions, bell peppers, spices (all available in-store!)

Steps: Blend tomatoes & peppers, fry with onions, add rice & stock, simmer until done.

Egusi Soup

Ingredients: Egusi seeds, spinach/ugwu, meat or fish, palm oil, peppers.

Steps: Grind egusi, cook with stock & oil, add greens & proteins, simmer.
